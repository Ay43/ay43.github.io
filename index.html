Intro

Hi there, Welcome to my page. My name is Adebayo S. Ayobami. I am a data enthusiast. I enjoy applying my data analysis skills to extract insights, from large datasets and visualize them to a meaningful story. I just love everything about data. My interests range from technology to design, programming, music and reading. By the way, check out my Projects.

Presidential Election (SQL)

2015 & 2019 (NIGERIA)

This Analysis focus on how APC and PDP were able to gathered votes across all states and geopolitical zones at the 2015 and 2019 Presidential Election in Nigeria.

This projects was done before the 2023 Presidential Election and the Objective is to help give insights on what is expected to play out in the 2023 election.

In this projects, I showed how Apc and Pdp were voted for in 2015 & 2019 Presidential Election across all the states in the country and the geopolitical zone.

I also showed an overview on the registered_voters, accredited_voters, votes_cast, valid_votes and rejected_votes across the states and geopolitical zones in the country. View Project.

Website Analysis (Excel)

The CEO and the web development team of a company that sells holidays (vacations) online through a website would like to know how their company website has performed over the months (Jan — Oct) in the year 2017.

In this projects, I showed how often users turned customers, the average time spent on the website, how the bounce rate occured, visits, pageviews and the most engaging contents on the website. View Project.
